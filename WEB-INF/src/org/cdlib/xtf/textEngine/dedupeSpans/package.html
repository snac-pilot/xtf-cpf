This package contains a heavily modified version of Lucene's "span" query/scoring structure, adapted to XTF's strategy of dividing large documents into small chunks.

To maintain the illusion of a single large contiguous document, it was necessary to overlap each chunk with the next chunk. Unfortunately, this opens the possibility of getting a span hit in the overlapping portion; the job of the DedupeQueue class is to detect and resolve such overlaps.

Lucene's Span classes (such as SpanNearQuery and its span generator, NearSpans) have been adapted to provide a score for each span, and minor modifications have been made to improve their behavior.

Finally, SpanScorer has been modified to report the final set of SpanHits to a SpanHitCollector, used by XTF to eventually form Snippets of source text.
